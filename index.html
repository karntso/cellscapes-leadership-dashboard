CellScapes Leadership Team: Rules of Engagement

+
How we behave individually and as a team; how we execute the operating principles
1. How we show up
  • Focus and deliver on defined goals, consistently and on time
  • Model leadership behavior
  • Be exceptional, not the exception
2. How we communicate
  • Be direct, clear, and "grown up"
  • Explore and respect preferences for format, timing, and place for communication
  • Respect colleagues' time and expertise, especially in staff meetings
  • Disagreement is welcome, undermining is not
3. How we handle conflict
  • Raise concerns directly with the person involved
  • Avoid passive-aggressive behavior (address issues openly, avoid venting downward)
  • Discuss and decide ownership based on defined priorities
  • Consensus is not required; we disagree and commit
4. External alignment
  • We will align on how we communicate externally

Brand FAQs
How should we be talking about ourselves moving forward?
No more "Allen Institute for X" brands. We now use a breadcrumb system: allen institute/brain science, allen institute/immunology, etc. Describe your work based on what you work on, not ownership or hierarchy — a shift from "I am" to "I work on."
Example: "I work at the Allen Institute, a non-profit research organization specializing in neuroscience, cell biology, synthetic biology, and immunology. I work on the Immunology team where we're researching x with a goal towards impact y."
Past institutes are now accelerators; smaller efforts are projects or initiatives. The word "team" remains appropriate in casual contexts.
Can we still use acronyms (e.g., AIBS, AIFI)?
The goal is to reduce acronyms, especially in external communications. Acronyms such as AIBS and AIFI should be phased out entirely.

Allen – investor, philanthropist, and co-founder of Microsoft. We gratefully acknowledge their vision and generosity, which make this work possible."

"Thank you to our founders, Jody Allen and the late Paul G. Allen, whose vision and generosity make our work possible."
How to refer to our organization when submitting a manuscript for publication
Use a comma between Allen Institute and the name of your accelerator: Allen Institute, <Name of accelerator>
